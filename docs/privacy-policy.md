# YT Notes Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

`YT Notes` stores the notes you write for YouTube videos on your device using Chrome's local extension storage.

## What YT Notes stores

- the YouTube video ID for the video you are taking notes on
- the video title and watch URL
- the notes you type
- the last-saved timestamp for the note

## How data is used

- to restore your notes when you revisit the same YouTube video
- to insert timestamps based on the current playback time
- to export your notes as a Markdown file when you choose `Export`

## Data sharing

`YT Notes` does not send your notes or video metadata to a remote server.

`YT Notes` does not sell, rent, or share your note data with third parties.

## Data retention and deletion

Your notes remain in local extension storage until you delete the extension or clear the extension's stored data through Chrome.

Any Markdown export you create is saved only when you choose to download it.

## Permissions

- `storage`: saves notes locally on your device
- `tabs`: identifies the active YouTube watch tab
- `scripting`: reads playback state and seeks the current video when needed
- `sidePanel`: opens the notes UI in Chrome's side panel
- `webNavigation`: keeps the side panel enabled only on YouTube watch pages during YouTube's single-page navigation
